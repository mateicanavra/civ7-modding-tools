## Current Boundary

Current survivor authority already closes domain roots, domain `ops/` roots,
and operation depth one. An operation presently requires `contract.ts` and
`index.ts`; `types.ts`, `rules/`, `strategies/`, and `policy/` remain admitted.
Existing domain-operation rules also protect runtime, adapter, projection,
config-facade, orchestration, contract-file, and registry boundaries.

Current Habitat capability law is settled before this descent:

- `RuleDiagnostics` owns vendor-neutral diagnostic demand and results;
- `RuleFixPreview` owns admitted, vendor-neutral preview impacts and never
  writes source;
- Nx owns ordering and deduplication for output-materializing tasks.

These are prerequisites, not A.2 design apertures.

## Asserted Future Boundary

One operation has this closed interior:

```text
ops/<operation>/
  contract.ts
  index.ts
  rules/
    index.ts
    <named-rule>.ts       # zero or more
  strategies/
    index.ts
    <named-strategy>.ts   # one or more
```

The clauses are positive and exact:

1. `contract.ts`, root `index.ts`, `rules/`, and `strategies/` are required.
2. Root `index.ts` is assembly-only. Behavior lives in named rule or strategy
   modules, not at the root.
3. `rules/index.ts` and `strategies/index.ts` are export-only and source only
   local named modules.
4. `rules/index.ts` may be empty when an operation has no named rule.
5. Every operation has at least one named strategy module; an inline root
   implementation is not a valid alternate shape.
6. Operation-local `types.ts`, `policy/`, helper buckets, schema/data buckets,
   nested directories, and other catchalls are outside the topology.
7. Strategy dependency law is capability-based: same-operation public
   surfaces, the owning domain's public model, and admitted public packages
   are legal classes. Private cross-domain and private sibling-operation
   reaches are forbidden. The law does not maintain a path inventory.
8. Behavior, configuration, runtime semantics, recipes, and public imports are
   invariant through the migration.

Contract schema-description quality is package-owned and exterior to A.2.
The six current metadata gaps do not widen this topology descent. Blanket
exported-function JSDoc and stale recipe-path clauses are retired rather than
re-homed.

## Selection Commitments

In:

- generic positive topology and dependency authority for all six domains;
- current operation-local files that conflict with the asserted topology;
- proxy rules whose concern is absorbed by the positive survivor;
- preservation of every behavior-bearing definition and public contract.

Exterior:

- recipe, stage, step, Studio, runtime control, mod-map, toolkit, and workspace
  topology;
- domain `model/` interior design;
- contract description completion and other schema-value quality;
- strategy algorithm, configuration, or selection redesign;
- live mutation through `RuleFixPreview`;
- any change to generated product behavior.

## Current Evidence Seed

The refreshed census sees 101 operation roots, zero missing contracts, 60
operation-local `types.ts` files, seven `policy/` directories, four `rules/`

## Falsifiers

- Advisory enforcement produces a class with no destination under the settled
  topology.
- A move would change behavior, configuration, public imports, or runtime
  semantics rather than preserve them.
- The generic dependency law cannot describe current legitimate use without a
  path inventory or broad exception.
- The proposed survivor cannot fail an injected representative of a proxy
  rule's claimed concern.
- A red row belongs to an exterior kind and cannot be separated cleanly.

Any falsifier stops that class and returns it to authority judgment. It does
not authorize an exception, new bucket, or quiet scope expansion.

## Split And First Use

This frame lands on the common split parent. A.2 and independent product work
open as sibling Graphite children. Only a real dependency justifies moving the
continuation's first unique branch above the A.2 tip.

The A.2 sibling's first state transition is authority implementation followed
by advisory row derivation. `execution/`, `receipts/`, and `ascent.md` are
created only when those states are actually entered.
