responsibilities. They intentionally cite current modules as evidence, not as
candidate bounded contexts.

## Flow Findings

- The current implementation routes many concerns through `command-engine.ts`,
  but scenario flows do not share one domain authority.
- `classify`, `check`, `verify`, `fix`, `hook`, and generators form a product
  workflow, but their proof classes differ and must remain explicit.
- Future authoring scenarios need their own context because current supported
  generators are intentionally narrow.
