# Phase 2 Goal

This is the exact objective to attach through `create_goal` after the preparation corpus passes adversarial review.

## Objective

Design the full Phase 2 Deep Habitat Toolkit refactor workstream packet suite, domino by domino, from the prepared source-authority register, scenario corpus, code topology map, domain-responsibility map, validation record, and reviewed domino ledger. Apply the authority order and conflict rules from `source-authority.md`. For every domino, first pass the packet-minimization gate, then produce a packet that states product workflow, scenario, domain owner, consumer, contract, dependency order, TypeScript state-space reduction, public surface impact, exact proof gates, review lanes, downstream realignment, Graphite/OpenSpec closure, and stop conditions. Preserve Habitat as a generic repo-local structural toolkit; do not implement packets until the suite is complete and reviewed.

## Attachment Conditions

Attach this goal only after:

- the full preparation corpus exists on disk,
- agents have reviewed the corpus adversarially,
- accepted P1/P2 findings are dispositioned,
- validation command results are recorded in `validation-results.md`,
- the worktree is on the expected Graphite branch and clean after commit.

Known current proof risks in `validation-results.md` do not block attaching this design goal because the goal is to design the packet suite, not claim implementation closure. They do block any Phase 2 packet from claiming proof closure unless fixed, explicitly rejected with evidence, or carried as a packet stop condition.

## Phase 2 Non-Goals

- Do not implement TypeScript refactors.
- Do not generate MapGen Authoring Topology.
- Do not treat proof classes as interchangeable.
- Do not collapse the packet suite into one general architecture note.
