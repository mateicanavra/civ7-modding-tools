# Resource measurements

**Executable authority:** [`families/resources.ts`](../../families/resources.ts)

This family follows each resource candidate from demand admission through plan,
adjustment, stamp outcome, and observed headless identity. It measures terminal
closure, typed reasons, habitat and phase fidelity, same-type spacing, authored
count ranges, regional minimums, geological pair correlation, landmass density,
15-degree latitude bands, and a 4 x 4 sector entropy surface.

Cross-landmass density rows retain every modeled landmass. The Earthlike product
target compares only samples with at least two landmasses that each contain 10%
or more of modeled land; this family does not require that geography or silently
turn a non-comparable sample into a pass.

Provisional authored ranges, latitude overrepresentation, and entropy remain
evidence until a target explicitly admits a product expectation. Headless policy
legality is not live-engine legality agreement.
