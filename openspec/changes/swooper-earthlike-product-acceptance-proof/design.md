## Design

Product acceptance consumes exact-authorship and parity proof. It is the first
place broad visible quality can close.

## Acceptance Rows

Rows must cover:

- mountain regions: footprint, length, width, internal mix, flat pockets,
  foothills, rough support, and passability;
- rivers and floodplains: visible networks, scoped floodplain placement, and
  hydrology/ecology truth ownership;
- resources and wonders: legal/age-appropriate distribution, spacing, diversity,
  and non-brittle wonder targets;
- starts: viable land or island networks, expansion paths, and readback proof;
- ecology and vegetation: richer vegetation without desert overcrowding;
- coasts/shelves/archipelagos: plausible coastal and island structure;
- Studio visualization: semantic colors, layers, legend/projection/orientation,
  and deploy confidence.

## Evidence Pairing

Each row needs same-run evidence: Studio visible state, diagnostics/stats,
generated/deployed identity, live readback or classified delta, and reviewer
disposition.

## Review Lanes

- Product/visual review.
- MapGen domain review by affected component.
- Studio visualization review where UI/projection is implicated.
- Proof-boundary review.
