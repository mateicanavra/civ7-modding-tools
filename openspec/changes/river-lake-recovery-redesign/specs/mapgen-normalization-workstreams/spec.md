## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: River And Lake Recovery Must Be Reframed Before Further Closure Claims

River and lake recovery SHALL be framed as an authority-grounded workstream
before additional closure claims are made for visible product success.

#### Scenario: Superseding objective is recorded

- **WHEN** the existing execution goal is narrower than the required product
  outcome or references stale workstream state
- **THEN** the workstream records a new durable objective with explicit owner
  boundaries, proof classes, and closure criteria
- **AND** later execution slices derive from that objective rather than from
  stale branch memory or chat state

### Requirement: River And Lake Recovery Planning Must Start From Physical Truth And Owner Boundaries

The redesign SHALL declare the physical expectations and owner boundaries before
it sequences new implementation slices.

#### Scenario: Execution change train is defined

- **WHEN** the workstream emits the next change sequence
- **THEN** each slice names the owning boundary, forbidden owners, expected proof
  gates, and stop conditions
- **AND** upstream Hydrology truth, Civ semantic parity, projection, parity
  proof, and product acceptance are kept as separate closure surfaces

### Requirement: No Visible-River Completion Claim Without Same-Run Product Proof

Visible-river completion SHALL require same-run product proof, not only code,
stats, readback, or terrain identity.

#### Scenario: A river slice claims completion

- **WHEN** a slice claims visible river or lake success in product reality
- **THEN** it includes same-run Studio-visible and Civ-rendered evidence or an
  explicit accepted out-of-scope disposition
- **AND** lower proof classes remain labeled separately
