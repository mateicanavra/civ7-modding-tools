## Why

Mountain-region quality remains a visible product risk. The intended object is
not a thin spine or line; it is a long, wide, physically grounded, internally
varied and passable region.

Direct product signal on 2026-06-06: the latest generated map is somewhat
better with mountains, but still visibly poor. The next repair should be a
holistic, layered, systematic pass that cares about physical realism, organic
aesthetic mountain regions, and playable organic movement instead of isolated
metric tuning.

## Activation Gate

This change is evidence-gated. Do not implement it until product acceptance or
parity proof records a current failing mountain-region row.

## What Changes

- Measure mountain regions as regions: footprint, length, width, diameter,
  connectedness, internal non-mountain share, flat pockets, peak share,
  foothill support, rough support, and passability.
- Repair only the proven owner: morphology controls, region axis planning,
  terrain projection, Studio visualization, or live materialization.
- Preserve physical gating through convergent boundaries, uplift, relief,
  history, or documented substrate.

## Stop Conditions

- The failing row cannot distinguish morphology truth, projection, Studio
  rendering, and live materialization.
- A fix would optimize spines while degrading region shape.
